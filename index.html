Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 March 2026

Bhajan Book is a free, offline-first app. We respect your privacy and collect as little data as possible.

What we collect

  • Nothing automatically. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect data.
  • Error reports and suggestions — only when you choose to submit one. These are sent via email and contain only the text you write plus the bhajan number for context. No device identifiers or personal data are attached.

What we do not collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No device identifiers or fingerprints
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or behavioural tracking
  • No cookies (this is a native app, not a website)

Data storage

All your data (playlists, preferences) is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. The app checks for catalogue updates from a public GitHub repository — this is a standard HTTPS request that does not transmit any personal data.

Third-party services

  • EmailJS — used only when you submit an error report or suggestion. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub — catalogue update checks download a small file from a public GitHub repository. See GitHub Privacy Statement.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or anyone else.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email bhajanbookapp@gmail.com.

Support

Found an error in the lyrics or transliteration? Have a suggestion for a bhajan to add?

Use the Report Error or Suggest Bhajan buttons within the app — they are available on every bhajan page and in Settings.

You can also email us directly at bhajanbookapp@gmail.com.
